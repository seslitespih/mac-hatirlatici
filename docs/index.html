Frequently Asked Questions

Which channels does the app cover?

The app covers all major Turkish sports channels including beIN Sports, TRT, EXXEN, S Sport, A Spor, and more.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Premium subscription at any time through the App Store: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Match Reminder → Cancel Subscription.

The match schedule is incorrect

Match data is updated daily. Please close and reopen the app to refresh. If the problem persists, contact us.

Privacy Policy

Match Reminder does not collect personal data. The app only uses your notification permission to send match reminders. See our Privacy Policy.
